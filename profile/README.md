# Chabad Commons

AI for and by Shluchim. Secure, free, open-source, and built for our shared future.

## What We Do

Chabad Commons is a space where shluchim can build together and exchange ideas with code. The idea is that when one shliach builds and shares something here, all other shluchim can benefit from it.

## GabAI

A personal Shlichus AI assistant that runs on WhatsApp and Slack, originally forked from [Nanoclaw](https://github.com/nanocoai/nanoclaw). GabAI helps shluchim with scheduling, answering questions, managing WhatsApp groups, voice transcription, CRM automation and more.

**[Request access](https://github.com/chabad-commons/.github/issues/new?template=access-request.yml)** to get started.


## Skills Marketplace

Browse, install, and share community-built AI skills. Share your custom skills via GitHub to help other shluchim.
